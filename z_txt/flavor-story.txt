Orcs are "mined" and pulled from the ground on certain locations on the map called pits.
Pits are marked be decaying vegetation and a dark, thick mud that forces the terrain into a shallow hole.

First time finding a pit:
"The air becomes increasingly thick. The stinking mud shifts and bubbles. Corrupted earth slowly sinks into itself,
bleeding the surrounding area of all life. This is where an empire will be forged."
